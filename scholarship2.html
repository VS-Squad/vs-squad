Need-Based Scholarship Program

Financial support for students demonstrating economic need

This scholarship is designed to assist students who demonstrate significant financial need and are committed to pursuing higher education despite economic challenges.

Application Deadline: July 15, 2025

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must demonstrate verifiable financial need (family income below $50,000 annually)
  • Must be a resident of the state for at least 12 months
  • Must be enrolled or accepted at an accredited college/university
  • Minimum 2.5 GPA (on 4.0 scale) for continuing students
  • Must complete at least 20 hours of community service per academic year

Required Documents

  • Completed scholarship application form
  • Official transcripts (high school or college)
  • Copy of most recent tax returns (student/parents)
  • Proof of income (pay stubs, W-2 forms, or other documentation)
  • Two letters of recommendation (one academic, one personal)
  • 500-word personal statement describing financial need and educational goals

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply if I'm an international student?

No, this scholarship is only available to U.S. citizens or permanent residents who meet the state residency requirements.

When will award decisions be made?

All applicants will be notified by August 31, 2025 via the email address provided in your application.

Can the scholarship be renewed?

Yes, recipients may reapply each year provided they maintain a 2.5 GPA and submit proof of continued financial need.
