Athletic Excellence Scholarship

Recognizing outstanding student-athletes who demonstrate excellence in sports and academics

This prestigious scholarship program supports talented student-athletes who have demonstrated exceptional performance in their sport while maintaining strong academic standing. Our goal is to help you achieve both your athletic and educational aspirations.

Application Deadline: August 1, 2025

Eligibility Criteria

  • Participation in state, national, or international level competitions within the last two years
  • Minimum cumulative GPA of 2.5 on a 4.0 scale
  • Enrollment or acceptance at an accredited college/university
  • Letter of recommendation from coach or athletic director
  • Commitment to continue participating in your sport at the collegiate level
  • Clean disciplinary record and good sportsmanship

Eligible Sports

Basketball

Football

Baseball

Volleyball

Swimming

Track & Field

Required Documents

  • Completed scholarship application form
  • Official transcripts (sealed or electronic)
  • Certificates of athletic achievements/competitions
  • Two letters of recommendation (one from coach, one academic)
  • Personal statement (500-750 words) outlining your athletic journey and goals
  • Recent game/competition video highlights (YouTube/Vimeo link)
  • Proof of college acceptance (for incoming freshmen)

Award Details

  • Amount: $5,000 - $15,000 (based on achievement level)
  • Renewable for up to 4 years with maintained eligibility
  • Access to athletic mentorship program
  • Invitation to annual scholar-athlete recognition banquet
