Artistic Scholarship

Celebrating creativity and artistic excellence

This prestigious scholarship recognizes and supports students who demonstrate exceptional talent and potential in the visual, performing, or literary arts.

Visual Arts Music Theater Dance Creative Writing Film

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must submit a portfolio of original creative work
  • Letter of recommendation from an art instructor or mentor
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5 (artistic merit is primary consideration)
  • Enrolled or accepted in an accredited institution
  • Demonstrated commitment to artistic development

Scholarship Benefits

  • $5,000-$10,000 award
  • Exhibition opportunity
  • Mentorship program
  • Access to masterclasses
  • Artist networking events
  • Featured in our annual showcase

Last Date of Submission

All applications must be received by September 10, 2025 at 11:59 PM EST

Documents Required

  • Portfolio: 5-10 samples of your best work (images, videos, audio files, or writing samples as appropriate)
  • Artist Statement: 500-1000 words describing your artistic practice and goals
  • Letter of Recommendation: From an art instructor or professional in your field
  • Academic Transcripts: Unofficial copies accepted
  • Resume/CV: Highlighting artistic achievements and exhibitions
