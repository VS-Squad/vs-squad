Academic Excellence Award

$5,000 Annual Award

This prestigious scholarship is awarded to students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance and leadership potential. Recipients will be recognized at the annual Honors Convocation and may be eligible for renewal.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum cumulative GPA of 3.5 on a 4.0 scale
  • Full-time enrollment in an accredited undergraduate program
  • Completion of at least 12 credit hours (for continuing students)
  • Demonstrated leadership in school or community activities

Application Deadline

June 30, 2025 (11:59 PM EST)

Documents Required

  • Official academic transcripts
  • Two letters of recommendation (one academic, one personal)
  • Personal statement (500-750 words) outlining academic goals and achievements
  • Resume or CV highlighting extracurricular activities and leadership roles

Selection Process

Applications will be reviewed by the Scholarship Committee based on academic achievement, leadership qualities, and the quality of the personal statement. Finalists may be invited for a virtual interview. Awards will be announced by August 15, 2025.
